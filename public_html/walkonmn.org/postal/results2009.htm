Final Results: 2009 1hr Postal & ½hr Postal

Paula Graham asked a good question, that merits an answer here. Why are there so many even numbers (for example, 12000m or 10000m) instead of more random numbers (for example 8997m)? Many of the results submitted come from races where only whole laps were counted, with any partial lap discarded. For instance, the race on 2009-08-16 was the USA 15k, and 1km splits were available, so the distance traveled is the number of km covered during the first hour, discarding the partial km, as there was no record of where people were at the hour mark. Some other races have similar simplifications.
