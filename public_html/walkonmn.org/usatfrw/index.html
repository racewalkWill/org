Video Coaching & Video Judging

USATF Racewalking Program from Walk On! Minnesota

 

Overview

The purpose of this program is to upgrade the coaching available to racewalkers in the USA. Technique coaching can be done by video analysis, even if the technique coach and the athlete are no in the same geographic location. Similarly, judging can determine if a walker's technique is legal or not, and if not, what is wrong.

 

How the Program Works

An athlete follows a few simple steps to register with the program, install the video analysis software, and create the video. The athlete then selects a video coach/judge, purchases a video coaching/judging session and uploads the video. The coach/judge downloads the video and returns feedback to the athlete.
