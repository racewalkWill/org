Definitions

Place - the order that people finished. The first finisher is 1.

Age Group - a code that gives the gender and the age range of the participant. Age groups that contain a number describe a 5 year age group. The word "open" is from age 14-34. For instance, M55 means Men, from age 55-59. Wopen means Women, from age 14-34.

Age Group Place - this is similar to Place, but applied only to the age group.

We are looking for other ways to score teams made of up walkers of different styles of walking (and hence different speeds) and for walkers going different distances. Our goal is to allow a team of any mix of walkers, and still give everyone a "fair" chance at being a part of a winning team.  The method used this year (the sum of the 3 best age-group places) meets this criteria.  If you have other suggestions, please e-mail them to us.  Thanks.
