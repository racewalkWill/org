Weather

A beautiful day for a race. Started out at 41F, clear sky with a light wind from the west. Temperatures climbed to 71F by mid afternoon, when it became partly cloudy. A halo was around the sun for much of the afternoon.

 

Pre-Race Publicity

The Saint Cloud Times had an article about our walking marathon a couple of weeks before the event. The couple featured in the article, Buzz and Kathy Snyder walked in this year's event.

 

Web Story from WJON

An article about the We Walk! Marathon appeared Sunday, including an interview with Anita Macias-Howard, the winner of the Half Marathon.

Terry Moore - Packet Pickup, Half Start, Finish Line, excess clothing bag pickup, transport for walkers abandoning the event, new volunteer training

 

Dave Daubert - event planning, t-shirt design, vendors, course setup on Friday and (after walking 50 km) 4 miles of course teardown on Saturday, logistics

 

Bruce Leasure - event planning, finisher medal design, registration, web site, course setup on Friday, starts, finish, finish line pictures, walk judge and course teardown on Saturday, results, logistics
