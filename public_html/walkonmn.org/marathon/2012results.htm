Suggestions for 2013

Please send us your suggestions and/or comments. Please send them to info@walkonmn.org. Here are the suggestions we have heard so far:

(1) get complete results out in a more timely manner.

(2) simplify/speedup packet pickup process

(3) add more port-a-potties (even if you have to raise prices a little)

(4) have the half marathon bus leave a little later (there was about 20 minutes of standing around time at the half marathon start)
