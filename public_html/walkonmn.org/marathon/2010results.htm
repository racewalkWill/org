We Walk! 2010 Results

 

2010 was the second year of the We Walk! Marathon, Half Marathon and 50km.

 

Many thanks to everyone that participated. Many of the volunteers commented on how friendly everyone was. Thanks for being nice to them. It makes it easier to recruit volunteers for next year.

 

The weather day was good, starting out with a typical May temperature of 45F but under sunny skies, warming to 70 degrees by noon. There was only a gentle breeze. Spring was in early this year, so almost all of the trees were in full leaf. The corn is all planted and just peeking up in some places. The cows were out, watching everyone go by.

 

We had 85 entrants, and 80 starters. This is more than 3 times as many people participating as last year. For the second year in a row, everyone that started, finished.

Judging

We had USATF certified racewalking judges on the course. If 3 walking judges agreed that someone was not racewalking, we moved them into the walk division. If any walking judge saw someone running (defined as having both shoes off the ground at the same time), we moved them into the walk/run division. If someone told us at the finish line (we did not ask) that they ran, even a little, we put them in the walk/run division.
