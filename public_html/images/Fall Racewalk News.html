Events - Oct 18th Gateway Trail & Nov 1 Lake Harriet
Twin Cities Race Walkers Fall 2015 E-mail
 
Hello Racewalkers !
 
We have some new subscribers to the mailing; to them it’s a big welcome to the fun of walking fast. As you gain experience, we hope you enjoy racewalking as much as the long time club members.
 
There are two more outdoor events on the calendar; both are excellent opportunities for some tips and coaching. Although we usually have at least one National level judge on the course, we all view it as a good time to improve our technique. After the races it’s common to gather at a nearby coffee shot to study the iPad slow motion playback and notice the finer points of our respective techniques.
 
Remember October 18th on the Gateway Trail is the next event – Come one, come ALL !!
 
Plus, keep the the closing ‘Fall Fling’ informal 5 km around Lake Harriet on November 1 on your calendar. Our format at this event is a handicap, slow or fast everyone has a good chance!! Just bring your estimate of your expected 5 km time and join in the video review at Brueggers coffee shop afterwards.
 
Details as usual at http://twincitiesracewalkers.org/events/
 
The winter schedule should be posted in December at the USA Track and Field Minnesota site http://usatfmn.org. This typically is a set of 5 indoor meets on local college tracks.
 
A new regular weekly walking group has started in Bloomington on Thursday mornings – 7:30 am from Ridgeview School into the Hyland Park Reserve. The St Paul and Minneapolis Weekly Walks continue at Como Lake and Lake Harriet on Wednesdays and Sundays. Newcomers always are welcome. See details at http://twincitiesracewalkers.org/weeklywalks.htm
 
Finally, a BIG congratulation to club members in both the National Senior Games this year and the National Youth Olympics. Our club has walked away with some great medals.  Please pat yourself on the back for the training and coaching that lead to such outstanding results.
 
Have Fun, Go Fast !
Will Loew-Blosser, for Twin Cities Race Walkers
