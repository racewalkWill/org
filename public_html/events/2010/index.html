Racewalking 101

A 1-hour introduction to racewalking - Details & Handout.

Offered once each month, May-September on the second Monday of the month.

6pm-7pm at Como Lake - meet at the Black Bear statue.

Cost: $5

 

Judging 101

You will be a USATF/MN Association level racewalk judge after successfully completing this course. Learn how to apply the two rules governing the technique of racewalking. Learn the mechanics of racewalk judging. Complete the paperwork involved in becoming a USATF certified official and racewalk judge.

Offered: 8am-Noon, April 18, at the TCRW Spring Fling.

Cost: No charge for the course, but USATF will charge you for a copy of the rules ($15), for becoming a member ($30), and for becoming a certified official ($15).

Pre-Register Only: contact Bruce Leasure 651-330-9355

 

Unjudged Racewalking Events

You can racewalk in almost any running road race you see advertised. You may not get any special recognition or award, but passing some slower runners near the finish feels pretty darn good. Charities Challenge always has a racewalk division in their road events.
