 Twin Cities Race Walkers is proud to host an Open-to-All 5km, 10km and 15km Race Walk as a part of Race Walk Championship Weekend.

 

This event is designed for anyone that is not eligible for one of the Championship events, or for anyone that desires a shorter distance. Results will show everyone that walked 5km, 10km, or 15km as a part of the Championship Weekend.

 

See the general information on the Race Walk Championship Weekend for more information.
