Awards - USA 15km Race Walking Championships

 

The awards ceremony will be held in the event hotel at approximately 9:30am after the race.

 

USA 15 km Race Walking Championships

USA championship medals to top 6 Open men and top 6 Open women

 

USA championship medals to the scoring members of the top 3 male teams and the top 3 female teams

  • Team membership is governed by the club membership identified in each athlete's USATF records as of the Saturday before the race
  • Teams shall consist of 3 or more members
  • The scoring members of a team are the top 3 finishers of that team
  • Team scoring is governed by USATF Rule 7 Paragraph 5 which requires scoring by time for race walking
  • In the event of a chip timing failure, scoring by place will be used. Non-scoring team members do not displace
 

USA 15 km Junior Race Walking Championships

USA championship medals to top 6 Junior men and top 6 Junior women

 

WMA North America, Central America and Caribbean Region 15 km Race Walk

Ribbons to top 3 master men and top 3 master women in 5 year age groups starting at 35

 

Open-to-All 5 km Race Walk

Ribbons to top 3 men and top 3 women

 

Open-to-All 10 km Race Walk

Ribbons to top 3 men and top 3 women 

 

Open-to-All 15km Race Walk

Ribbons to top 3 men and top 3 women

 

20 km Race Walk for Elite and Near-Elite

No awards, but we will announce your times.
