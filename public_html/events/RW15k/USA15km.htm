 Twin Cities Race Walkers is proud to host the 2010 edition of the USA 15 km Race Walking Championships as one of the events of Race Walk Championship Weekend.

 

Participation in the USA 15km Race Walking Championships is limited by USATF Rules Article I, Section I, Rule 1 and the definition of Open on the previous page of the rules to those eligible to represent the United States in all levels of international competition. Participants must be a citizen of the USA and at least 14 years old on the day of competition. Participants must be a member of USATF.

 

Prize Money - We are pleased to announce $5,000 of prize money this year.  details

 

See the general information on the Race Walk Championship Weekend for more information.

The USA 15km Race Walking Championships is part of the USA Race Walking Grand Prix, a season long competition combining results from 13 different competitions, across a range of distances from 1 mile to 50km.
