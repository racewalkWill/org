USA 15 km Race Walking Championships

USA 15 km Junior Race Walking Championships

 

WMA North America, Central America and Caribbean Region 15km Race Walking Championships

 

Open-to-All 5 km, 10 km and 15 km Race Walks

 

20km Race Walk for Elite and Near-Elite

 

All of the above events start at 6:30am on Sunday, August 15, 2010 on the Greenway Trail directly behind the event hotel.

 

 

The USA 15km Race Walking Championships is part of the USA Race Walking Grand Prix, a season long competition combining results from 13 different competitions, across a range of distances from 1 mile to 50km.

Health Notice

Weather can be hot and humid and dehydration is a danger. Please hydrate properly prior to, during and after the race. Water and electrolyte replacement sports drink will be available on the course for all participants. 15km athletes may bring their own food/hydration for consumption during the race - bring a small sign to identify your stuff.

Things to Do

 Maybe these are just what you need to bring that non-race-walker in your life out to an event!

The event hotel is located in a trendy section of Minneapolis, with a variety of small restaurants and shops within easy walking distance.

A short light rail ride away:

  • downtown Minneapolis and the Nicollet Mall, 

  • the Mall of America - the USA's largest enclosed shopping mall

  • the Water Park of America, USA's largest indoor water park.

For more information on the greater Twin Cities area, try Minneapolis.org, or Saint Paul Convention & Visitor's Bureau. For information on greater Minnesota, try Explore Minnesota.
