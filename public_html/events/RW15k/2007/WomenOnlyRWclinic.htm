Instructor

Amber Antonia. An elite US Race Walker. An ACE Certified Elite Personal Trainer & Coach. Amber says "I love to have fun, so I intend this clinic to be interactive with lots of learning and laughing." Check out Amber's flyer to learn a little more about her.

 

Amber was part of the Team USA at the Race Walking World Cup in La Coruna, Spain. Here is her report from the 20km.

 

"The World Cup is one of the largest racewalking events in the world, with representation from 63 countries. The women’s 20K was held on May 13th and was won by Ryta Turova. My race took an unexpected turn when I found a big hole on the course at 8 kilometers . After stepping in this hole and taking a fall, I was unable to stand on my leg. I had a big DNF and had to spend the rest of my time in the medical tent. Not what I intended on doing in Spain. I am still in pain and have been a cross training maniac."
