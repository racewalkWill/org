Results

 

Twin Cities Race Walkers is proud to host the 2006, 2007 and 2008 USA 15km Race Walking Championships for Junior and Open athletes. Along with these national championship events, the USATF North Region 15km Race Walking Championships, the USATF Mid-America Region 15km Race Walking Masters Championships and the USATF/MN Association 15km Race Walking Championships will be held. The Canada vs. USA 10km Race Walking Junior Dual meet will also be contested, bringing 24 of the fastest 14-19 year old race walkers from those countries to the course. For those interested in a shorter distance, a 3km race walk open to everyone will be held shortly after the completion of the 15km.

 

 

The USA 15km Race Walking Championships is part of the USA Race Walking Grand Prix, a season long competition combining results from 13 different competitions, across a range of distances from 1 mile to 50km.

Course

 USATF Course Certification Number MN-05045-RR

A 2km loop on a straight, flat, smooth, asphalt section of the Greenway Trail with no road crossings and no vehicle traffic. The Greenway Trail used to be a rail road track going into the heart of Minneapolis. The trail is below grade, with an overpass at every block, providing ideal locations for spectators, and keeping traffic and exhaust well away from the course. Details

Getting there by light rail: The event hotel van will take you to the light rail stop at the Mall of America, or you can walk there yourself. The Mall of America is the end of the line, so there is only one way to go. Get off the light rail at Lake Street. Go west on Lake Street, away from Hiawatha Ave (the street the light rail parallels) for about 10 blocks to 13th Ave S. Turn right onto 13th Ave S. and slightly more than one block north is the trail entrance and the start/finish line. Here are directions from Google Maps.

Getting there by car: Just west of the event hotel is MN-77. Turn right onto MN-77 going north. Bear right onto Cedar Ave S (you are still going north). Turn left (west) when you get to E. Lake St. Go about 7 blocks to 13th Ave S. Turn right onto 13th Ave S. and slightly more than one block north is the trail entrance and the start/finish line. Here are directions from Google Maps. Google maps shows driving west on E 29th St, but that street is one-way to the east. Park on the streets. Be sure to put valuables in your trunk and lock your car. There is an entrance to the course at the corner of 13th Ave S and E 29th St. in Minneapolis.

Health Notice

Weather can be hot and humid and dehydration is a danger. Please hydrate properly prior to, during and after the race. Water and electrolyte replacement sports drink will be available on the course for all participants. 15km athletes may bring their own food/hydration for consumption during the race.
