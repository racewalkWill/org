Event Hotel - USA 15km Race Walking Championships

 

Sheraton Minneapolis Midtown Hotel

2901 Chicago Avenue South

Minneapolis, MN 55407

(866) 837-4196

 

$99/night if reserved by July 27, 2010 and ask for the "Race Walk" group rate.

 

The hotel does not provide shuttle service to or from the airport. You must take a taxi, take the light rail and walk about a mile, or rent a car.

 

The start/finish line is directly behind the hotel on the Greenway Trail.

 

Need a roommate to reduce expenses?

Drop us an e-mail and we'll try to put you in touch with someone else that wants to share a room.

 

 

Nearest Airport: MSP
