 Twin Cities Race Walkers is proud to host a 20 km Race Walk for Elite and Near-Elite race walkers as one of the events of Race Walk Championship Weekend.

 

Participation in the 20 km Race Walk for Elite and Near-Elite is limited to those that can complete 15km in under 1:30 and complete 20km in under 2:00. Participants attempting 20km will not be allowed to start the final 5km if they complete the first 15km after 8am (1:30 after the start of the 15km). No times will be published for anyone completing the 20km after 8:30am.

 

Other than the above restriction on based upon achieved speed during the first 15km, this event is open to all.

 

See the general information on the Race Walk Championship Weekend for more information.
