Race Walking 101

A 1-hour introduction to race walking - Details & Handout.

Offered once each month, May-September on the second Monday of the month.

6pm-7pm at Como Lake - meet at the Black Bear statue.

Cost: $5

 

Judging 101

You will be a USATF/MN Association level race walk judge after successfully completing this course. Learn how to apply the two rules governing the technique of race walking. Learn the mechanics of race walk judging. Complete the paperwork involved in becoming a USATF certified official and race walk judge.

Offered: 8am, April 19, at the TCRW Spring Fling.

Cost: No charge for the course, but USATF will charge you for a copy of the rules ($15), for becoming a member ($30), and for becoming a certified official ($15).
