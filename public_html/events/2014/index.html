Offered once each month, April-September on the second Monday of the month: April-September: April 8, May 13, June 10, July 8, August 12 and September 9 in 2013.

6pm-7pm at Como Lake - meet at the Black Bear statue.

Unjudged Racewalking Events

You can racewalk in almost any running road race you see advertised. You may not get any special recognition or award, but passing some slower runners near the finish feels pretty darn good. Charities Challenge always has a racewalk division in their road events.
