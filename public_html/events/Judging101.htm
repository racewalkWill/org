What You'll Learn

  • what makes race walking different from running
  • the rules of race walking
  • how to observe race walkers
  • the process used in judging
  • practice - you'll judge your first race
  • USATF certification - the union card for race walk judges

 

When & Where

Saturday, Apr 14 7:30am in the lobby of the Sheraton Minneapolis Midtown Hotel, 2901 Chicago Avenue South

Minneapolis, MN 55407 - before the USATF/MN 5km Race Walking Championships. You'll get some basic instruction on judging before the race, then judge the race! Then we'll retire to a local eatery for coffee/soda/water and do the paperwork to get you certified as a USATF/MN Association Level Race Walk Judge.

 

What to Bring

A pencil/pen and your checkbook. We will provide everything else.

 

Cost

$30.
