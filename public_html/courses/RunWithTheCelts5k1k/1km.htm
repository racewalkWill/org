Run With The Celts - 1km

 

 

 

General description of the 1 km course

  • Start at the 5 km finish line facing west, getting ready to run alongside the finish chute.
  • Turn left at the first sidewalk away from the river
  • Turn left at the first sidewalk, head towards ticket booth.
  • Keep the ticket booth on your left as you turn left
  • Go between the river and the big trees
  • Keep going until you get back to the 5 km finish line.
  • You've now done 500 meters.
  • Do 2 laps and you've done 1 km.

 

Volunteers: one smile and point volunteer at each corner. Two volunteers may be needed near the ticket booth. If very young children are involved, have parents run/walk with them, or place more volunteers along the path.

 

Cones: adjust the cones around the big trees just after the ticket booth to accommodate this course too.

 

Safety: imake sure any power cables crossing the course are not a trip hazard.

 

Visibility: parents can stand in the middle of the loop and watch their children the entire time.

 

Options: select 500 meters or 1 km as the distance.
