Run with the Celts 5k

 

 

 

General description of the 5 km course

  • Detail on Start
    • Start on Dr Justus OHage Blvd southwest of the intersection with Plato Blvd. The starting line is between the southwest edge of the storm drains on both sides of the street about 4 street lights southwest of Plato Blvd.
    • Go southwest on Dr Justus OHage Blvd continuing on to Water St W, using the whole road.
  • Detail on 1km
    • between 10 and 11 utility poles past the high bridge is the 1km mark
  • Detail on 1mile + West Turnaround + 2km
    • When Water St W does a sharp right/left jog, use only the left lane.
    • Continue west on Water St W past a small stone bridge
    • The 1 mile mark is at the big transmission tower.
    • Continue west on Water St W until a bike path leaves the road on the right
    • The West Turnaround is located at a seam in the pavement between the two metal posts at the bike path entrance
    • Turning toward the river, crossing into the other lane and head towards the start in that lane
    • Continue east on Water St W past the little stone bridge
    • Enter a bike path along the river side of Water St W at first opportunity.
    • At the end of the wooden trestle is the 2km smark
    • Continue on the bike path all the way back to the start.
  • Detail on Water Stop + 3km
    • The water stop is located at the entrance to the Saint Paul Yacht Club
    • Just past the road to the Saint Paul Yacht Club is the 3km mark
  • Detail on 2mile + Cross Over
    • When the path splits, take the right hand path
    • The 2 mile mark is in the bike path, 5 trees after the path splits
    • Cross Dr Justus OHage Blvd at the northeast crosswalk at the intersection of Plato Blvd.
    • Take an immediate left onto the first path (the walking path)
  • Detail on 4km
    • After the bridge over the park entrance, follow the left path
    • The 4km mark is just into the slow bend to the right.
    • Just before Wabasha Bridge, take the path off the levee to the left down to the sidewalk along Levee St
  • Detail on East Turnaround
    • Take the sidewalk along Levee St under the bridge to the last driveway before Water St E.
    • The East Turnaround is at the last driveway on Levee St before Water St E.
    • Swinging wide around a metal sign, using the driveway, then turn left into Levee St going back towards Wabasha Bridge
    • Go west on Levee St all the way to the end.
  • Detail on 3mile
    • The 3 mile mark is at the beginning of the drop-off lane on the right at the last parking lot
    • Go up the last ramp onto the riverside sidewalk and continue west
    • Go between the river and the large trees on the riverside sidewalk
  • Detail on Finish
    • Finish at the east edge of the 4th bench east of the first sidewalk away from the river.
