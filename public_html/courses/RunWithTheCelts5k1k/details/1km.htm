All along Water St. W

Cones: place cones in or around all pot holes. You may not see them all from a vehicle - it is recommended that a runner or biker check the course.

 

1 km split

Approximate Location: on Water St W, between 10th and 11th utility poles further away from the starting line than the high bridge carrying Smith Ave crosses high over Water St W. The road veers gently to the left. A wide gravel opening allows access to the bike trail on the right.

Location: a washered-PK nail in Water St W, about 1 foot from the left (south) edge of the road.

Signage: place the 1 km split sign on the right (north) side of the road, as the shortest path for the runners is on the left side of the road.
