2007 Run with the Celts 5km

 

USATF Certified Course MN-07030-RR.

 

Course Description

This course starts on Lilydale Rd (sometimes called Water St) underneath the center of the Smith Avenue high bridge. The course proceeds south west, on the street to Lilydale Regional Park. The course continues south west long the road, but is restricted to the left hand lane of the road. The 1 km mark is directly underneath the high utility lines that cross the river. The course continues in the left hand lane all the way to the 2 km mark. The 2 km mark is past the wooden trestle at the driveway of the large parking lot on the left separating the road and a pond. This is the turnaround. At the turnaround, the course doubles back on itself, staying in the left hand lane to the 3 km mark, which is directly across the road from the 1 km mark. The course continues north east in the left hand lane of the street until entering a bicycle/walk/run path that is closer to the river than the road at Lilydale Regional Park. The course continues north east on the bicycle/walk/run path. The 4 km mark is underneath the Smith Avenue high bridge, adjacent to the start. The course continues on the bicycle/walk/run path, past the yacht club up to Plato Boulevard. The course then turns left, down the sidewalk toward the river, crossing the parking lot entrance. After passing the sidewalk at the far end of the traffic circle, the course takes the next sidewalk to the north east, which merges into the sidewalk along the river. The course ends on the river sidewalk at the flag pole in the center of Harriet Island Park.

Exact Locations of Course Marks

 

Start - a PK nail in the southern most edge of Lilydale Rd, directly under the center of the Smith Avenue high bridge. Across the road, and across the bicycle/walk/run path from the 4 km mark.

 

1 km mark - a PK nail in the southern most edge of Lilydale Rd, before you cross the stone bridge, directly underneath the high power transmission lines that cross the river. Across the road from the 3 km mark.

 

1 mile mark - a PK nail in the southern most edge of Lilydale Rd, two light poles and 35' past the wooden trestle, away from the start line.

 

2 km mark & turnaround - a PK nail in the center of Lilydale Rd, 11' away from the start line than the utility pole at the north east parking lot entrance on the pond side of the road.

 

3 km mark - a PK nail in the northern most edge of Lilydale Rd, after the stone bridge, and before the wiggle in the road where the bike path starts, directly underneath the high power transmission lines that cross the river. Across the road from the 1 km mark.

 

2 mile mark - a PK nail in the northern most edge of the bicycle/walk/run path, 5 utility poles and 15' towards the finish line from where the bicycle/walk/run path starts at Lilydale Regional Park.

 

4 km mark - a PK nail in the northern most edge of bicycle/walk/run path, directly under the eastern edge of the Smith Avenue high bridge. Across the bicycle/walk/run path, and across the road from the start line.

 

3 mile mark - a PK nail at the southern most edge of the sidewalk, after the final 90 degree corner heading toward the finish. It is 3 sidewalk sections before the first cross sidewalk. The PK nail is in the dirt, not in the sidewalk itself.

 

Finish Line - extend the line from the center of the flag pole in the middle of Harriet Island Park through the center of the street light adjacent to the flag pole, across the sidewalk.

 

Required Cones

  • from where the bicycle/walk/run path joins Lilydale Rd. at Lilydale Regional Park, down the center of the road, all the way to the turnaround - about 1.1km
  • on the PK nail that marks the turnaround

Recommended Cones

  • across Lilydale Rd, just south east of the yacht club parking lot entrance - to close the Lilydale Rd to the south east (this is just before the start)
  • across the entrance to Lilydale Regional Park
  • across Lilydale Rod, just south east of the turnaround - to close Lilydale Rd
  • at the yacht club entrance near the start - to keep people parking at the yacht club lot from crossing the course
  • across each harbor access sidewalk from the 4km split to Plato Boulevard - to make pedestrians aware that something is going on
  • at the branch in the bicycle/walk/run path between the yacht club parking lot entrance and Plato Boulevard - to encourage runners to take the shorter left hand path.
  • at the entrance to the parking lot just prior to mile 4 - to keep vehicle traffic from crossing the course
  • at the 90 degree turn just before the 3 mile mark - to keep runners/walkers on the course

 

Recommended Water Stop Location

Just after the course enters the bicycle/walk/run path and before the 2 mile mark. There is a wide spot in the bicycle/walk/run path and there is convenient parking for volunteers in the driveway to Lilydale Regional Park.
