Course Description

 

The Central Race Walk Course is on the walking path around Bennett Lake, Central Park, Roseville, MN. This delightful loop course featuring 12 foot wide asphalt path that is flat, not crowned, pothole free, and has no sharp corners. This lightly used pathway has no automobile or truck traffic and no road crossings, but does cross a creek with a waterfall. 

 

We use the north side of the lake to build a 1km loop - and ideal distance for shorter races.

 

The course will be certified before the April 12, 2008 race.

 

 

Course Amenities

 

  • The park provides port-a-potties year-round.
  • We will provide water both on-course and post-race.
  • For 10km and longer events, we will provide a table course-side where you can stash your own on-course snacks and sports drinks. Please bring a small sign to identify your goodies.
  • We encourage you to bring your own water bottle and snacks for after the race.

 

 

Course Location, Directions, Map

 

Directions to the Course & Parking

Take the Lexington Ave exit from US36

Turn north on Lexington Ave

Before reaching County Rd C, park on the east side of the road.

The lake is immediately east of the parking lot, and the course begins on the path next to the lake and goes clockwise around the north edge of the lake..
