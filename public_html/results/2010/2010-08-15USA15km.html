Race Walk Championship Weekend

August 15, 2010, Minneapolis, MN

 

Race Start Time: 6:40am

Weather at Start: 65F, clear sky, 10mph wind from west

 

Course Certification: MN-10034-RR

Course Type: 2.5km out-and-back loop

Aid Station: located in middle of loop, serving walkers in both directions

 

Chief Judge: Diane Graham-Henry (IAAF)

Judges: Wayne Armbrust (Master), Tom Boshart (Association), Bruce Leasure (Master), Ginger Mulanax (IAAF), Tish Hanna (IAAF)

Splits

 

A timing mat was placed across both the east bound lane and the west bound lane at the start/finish point of the course. The eastern part of the course is traversed first on each lap (1500m). The western part of the course is then traversed (1000m). This means the first split time is for 1500m and the second split time is for 1000m. Add the two times together to get the lap time. This pattern repeats itself for each lap of the 2500m loop.
