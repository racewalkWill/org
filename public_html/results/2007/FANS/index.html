FANS is a 24 hour event. The object is to see how far you can run or walk in 24 hours. Two local walkers, Dave Daubert and Barb Curnow, and 3 walkers from out of town, Marshall King, Larry Block and Ollie Nanyes, competed. For their stories, go to htpp://groups.yahoo.com and look at the ultrawalking group. Complete results are on the FANS website: http://fans24hour.org/.
